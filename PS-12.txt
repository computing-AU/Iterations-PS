Full Diamond (Hollow)

A hollow diamond is two hollow triangles joined together. 
Top half: rows 1 to N. Bottom half: rows N-1 down to 1. Only print stars on the border of each row.

For N = 4:

*     *

(c)  Write the complete code for the full hollow diamond.  [2 marks]
