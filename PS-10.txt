Hollow Rectangle

A hollow rectangle prints stars only on the border. Interior cells are spaces. Size is N rows by 2N columns.

For N = 4:
********
*      *
*      *
********

Hint:	Inside the nested loop, use an if statement to decide whether to print * or a space. 
Ask: is this cell on the first row, last row, first column, or last column?

(a)  Write the condition that is true only when a cell should be a star (on the border).  [1 mark]
(b)  Write the complete nested loop code.  [2 marks]
(c)  Test your condition by hand:
Is cell (row=2, col=1) a star or a space for N=4? Is cell (row=2, col=4) a star or a space?  [1 mark]
